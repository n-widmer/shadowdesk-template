# Meeting Summary Template

Use this structure for all meeting summaries. Omit sections that don't apply (see the matrix at the bottom).

## Template

```
# [Meeting Type] Summary - [Client Full Name]

[date stamp: M/D/YY - HH:MM]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM EST
**Participants:** [Name (Company/Role)] for each person
**Type:** [Discovery Call / Coaching Session / Check-in / Demo / Follow-up / Proposal Review / Onboarding]
**Duration:** ~[X] minutes
**Platform:** [Google Meet / Zoom / Phone / In-person]
**Full transcript:** [relative link to transcript file, e.g., ../transcripts/YYYY-MM-DD-discovery-transcript.md]

---

## Call Outcome

[1-3 sentences. The verdict. What happened? What was decided? How warm/cold is the deal?]

---

## Key Takeaways

- [3-7 bullet points. The most important things from this meeting.]
- [Be specific: names, numbers, dates, tools, decisions.]
- [Each bullet should stand alone as useful information.]

---

## Pain Points Identified

- [Only include if new pain points surfaced in THIS meeting]

---

## What Resonated / Landed Well

- [Value props, demos, or ideas that got a positive reaction]
- [Include their reaction: "He said 'I'm in for sure'" not just "He liked it"]

---

## Notable Quotes

> "[Direct quote that reveals intent, emotion, or commitment level]"

> "[Another quote if relevant]"

[2-4 quotes max. Choose the ones that would help the user remember the vibe of the meeting.]

---

## Decisions Made

| Decision | Context |
|----------|---------|
| [What was decided] | [Why / what led to this] |

---

## New Information Gathered

- [Anything learned about the client, their company, tech stack, team, etc.]
- [Personal details worth remembering (kids, hobbies, connections)]
- [Competitive intel, market observations, referral opportunities]

---

## Next Steps

### You
- [ ] [Specific action] (by [date if applicable])

### [Client Name]
- [ ] [Specific action] (by [date if applicable])

---

## Risk Factors

- [Things that could slow down or kill the deal/project]
- [Budget concerns, timeline issues, decision-maker access, competitive threats]

---

## Session Plan (for recurring meetings only)

**Next session:** [Date, time, platform]
**Focus areas:** [What to cover next time]
**Prerequisites:** [What needs to happen before next session]
```

## Section Usage by Meeting Type

Include a section only when it applies. "Always" means always include it. "Sometimes" means include if relevant content exists. "Rarely/Never" means skip unless there's a strong reason.

| Section | Discovery | Coaching | Check-in | Demo | Proposal Review | Onboarding | Follow-up |
|---------|-----------|----------|----------|------|-----------------|------------|-----------|
| Call Outcome | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always |
| Key Takeaways | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always |
| Pain Points | Always | Rarely | Sometimes | Sometimes | Rarely | Never | Sometimes |
| What Resonated | Always | Sometimes | Rarely | Always | Always | Sometimes | Sometimes |
| Notable Quotes | Always | Sometimes | Rarely | Sometimes | Always | Rarely | Sometimes |
| Decisions Made | Sometimes | Always | Sometimes | Rarely | Always | Sometimes | Sometimes |
| New Information | Always | Sometimes | Sometimes | Sometimes | Sometimes | Always | Sometimes |
| Next Steps | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always | Always |
| Risk Factors | Always | Rarely | Sometimes | Rarely | Always | Rarely | Sometimes |
| Session Plan | Never | Always | Sometimes | Never | Never | Never | Never |
