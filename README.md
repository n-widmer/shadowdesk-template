# ShadowDesk OS

**Your AI Chief of Staff. Compounds every week.**

## What this is

This is your ShadowDesk OS — a folder on your laptop that holds your identity, your voice, your tools, and a running history of how you work. Every time you use it, it gets sharper.

## Start here

Once this folder is open in VS Code with Claude Code in the chat panel:

1. Go to **[shadowdesk.ai/levelup](https://shadowdesk.ai/levelup)** and enter your passcode.
2. Copy the whole bundle on that page.
3. Paste it into the chat panel and hit enter.

That kicks off Day One — about 30 minutes, guided by whoever is helping you set up. By the end you'll have your identity captured, two key settings dialed in, your business loaded so Claude knows who it's working with, and a clear handoff into building your first skill.

## If you're stuck

Just say "this is broken" or "this is frustrating." Claude will log what went wrong and ask what would have worked better. Your ShadowDesk OS gets sharper every time you tell it where it falls short.
